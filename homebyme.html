Explore Debating Formats

Every debate has its rules of engagement. We provide in-depth guides and practice arenas for the most popular parliamentary styles.

British Parliamentary

A dynamic, four-team format where two teams represent the Government and two represent the Opposition. It emphasizes quick thinking and strategic role fulfillment.

  • Teams: 4 teams of 2 speakers.
  • Speech Length: 7 minutes.
  • Structure: Opening and Closing halves for each side.
  • Key Feature: Points of Information (POIs) are crucial for engagement.

Asian Parliamentary

A classic two-team format that pits a Government against an Opposition. This style emphasizes direct clash and deep, principled argumentation.

  • Teams: 2 teams of 3 speakers.
  • Speech Length: 7 minutes.
  • Structure: 3 constructive speeches per side, plus a Reply Speech.
  • Key Feature: Reply speeches (4 mins) summarize the debate from a biased perspective.
